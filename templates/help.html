How to Use Rep Forge

Create Workout

Use the chatbot icon in the dashboard to generate your daily workout routine.

Track Progress

Your workout history will be stored automatically after pressing the "Done" button.

Update Profile

Use the "Manage Profile" button in the dashboard to update your personal and fitness details.

Notifications

Click the bell icon on the dashboard to toggle email and WhatsApp notifications on or off.

Need Assistance?

If something is not working, restart the app or contact support at repforge2007@gmail.com
